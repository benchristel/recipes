Ethiopian Lentils

Serves: a bunch

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • 1 onion
  • 4–5 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp fenugreek
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 lb red lentils (about 2 1/2 cups)
  • 2 small carrots or one large one
  • 1.5 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bunch kale

Procedure

  1. Sift through the lentils and pick out any stones.
  2. Sauté finely chopped onion in a couple teaspoons of oil until browned.
  3. Add minced or pressed garlic and sauté 30 seconds more.
  4. Add spices; sauté 30 seconds.
  5. Add lentils, diced carrots and sweet potatoes, and water to cover.
  6. Bring to a boil and simmer 30 minutes or until sweet potatoes are just cooked through.
  7. Stir in chopped kale and simmer 5 minutes or until kale is cooked.
